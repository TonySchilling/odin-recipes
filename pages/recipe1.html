Carbonara

This is a lovely dish best served hot!

Ingredients:

  • 1 packet of spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1l of whole milk
  • 1 slab of ham
  • 2 pounds of cheese
  • 1 pot of water for boiling

Ingredients:

  1. Bring water to a boil and add spaghettit
  2. In a stainless steel pan add all other ingredients
  3. Add cooked spaghetti
  4. Optional: Garnish with parsley
  5. Enjoy!!
