Borscht

This is a lovely dish from the heart of Ukraine!

Ingredients:

  • 4 large beets
  • 1 pound of veggies
  • 1l of chicken stock
  • 1 kielbasa
  • 2 pounds of cabbage
  • Water

Ingredients:

  1. Sautee all ingredients in butter
  2. Add broth and bring to a simmer
  3. Add water and cook until boiling
  4. Add dill
  5. Serve hot!!
